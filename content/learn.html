Three flavors of ARSnova

Whether you want to try ARSnova in the cloud or run it on your own machine, ARSnova offers a way to suit your needs.

Software as a Service

The easiest and fastest way is to use our Software as a Service offering at arsnova.eu. To get you started, we suggest you take a look at the manual which will walk you through. We suggest you use ARSnova side-by-side while you read the manual. If you would like stay updated on the latest news, please take a look at our blog.

Virtual environment

Are you interested in running ARSnova on our own machine? Then try our virtual environment which installs and configures the latest version of ARSnova along with all required services. This is the fastest way to get you up and running. Additionally, once you no longer want to use this environment, you can simply delete it without a trace.
